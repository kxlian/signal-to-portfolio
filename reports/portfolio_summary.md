## Interpretation

- `naive_signal` allocates capital in proportion to positive signal scores.
- `rank_based` ignores signal magnitude after ranking and equally weights the top names.
- `risk_aware` keeps the portfolio fully invested while balancing signal exposure against covariance risk and maximum position size.

The main research point is that a signal ranking alone is not an implementable portfolio. It needs sizing logic, constraints, and diagnostics before it becomes a decision-ready allocation.
